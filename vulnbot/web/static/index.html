Safety boundary

Do not exceed the authorized scope

The full VulnBot web interface presents only_*, blocked_*, and action constraints as readable boundaries, then shows blocked attempts and reasons on the safety boundary page.

For routine self-testing, specify at least one host, port, or path boundary.
